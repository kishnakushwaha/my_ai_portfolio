Remote AI/ML Internships This Week

Oct 10, 2025 • 5 min read
← Previous Article All Articles Next Article →

Moving from following tutorials to actually writing code for a company can seem like a huge leap. The good news is that internships make this transition easier. They give you a chance to learn, make mistakes, and see how AI works in real-world situations, all while getting paid. This week, there are more global remote opportunities than usual, especially for beginners. Here’s a list of remote AI/ML internships you can apply for now.

Remote AI/ML Internships

Whether you’re interested in Speech Recognition roles in India or LLM Forensics positions in Canada, here’s a list of remote AI/ML internships to help you start your career:

  1. SuperKalam – AI/ML Research Intern (India)
  2. Lexsi Labs – AI Research Intern (India)
  3. Sony – Speech Recognition Intern (India)
  4. Experian – AI/ML Intern (USA)
  5. OnePay – AI Research Intern (USA)
  6. Honeywell – AI/ML Intern (USA)
  7. TWG Global – AI/ML Scientist Intern (UK)
  8. Alignerr – Research Intern (UK)
  9. Redis – AI Research Intern (Canada)
  10. GPTZero – ML Intern (Canada)

Closing Thoughts

When you look at this list, avoid sending the same resume to all 10 positions.

If you enjoy math, focus on TWG. If you’re interested in systems and speed, consider Redis. And, if you care about products and users, SuperKalam could be a good fit.
