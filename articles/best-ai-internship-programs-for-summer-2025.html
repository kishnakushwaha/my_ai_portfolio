Best AI Internship Programs for Summer 2025

Oct 17, 2025 • 5 min read
← Previous Article All Articles Next Article →

Artificial Intelligence isn’t the future anymore, it is the present. Every sector is on the lookout for top-tier AI talent, from financial giants and telecom leaders to entertainment and fashion startups. And if you’re aiming to break into this rapidly growing space, there’s no better launchpad than a high-impact AI internship. So, in this article, I’ll highlight 5 of the best AI internship programs for Summer 2025 across India, the US, the UK, Canada, and Australia.

Best AI Internship Programs for Summer 2025

Whether you’re a student, researcher, or aspiring AI engineer, below are 5 of the best AI internship programs for Summer 2025.

Machine Learning Intern at Tower Research Capital

Location: Gurgaon (India), Domain: Algorithmic Trading, Predictive Modeling, ML Engineering.

Tower Research Capital is one of the most prestigious firms in the quantitative finance world. As a Machine Learning Intern, you’ll work on real-time prediction models, feature engineering for financial data, and backtesting trading strategies using ML pipelines. This is not just a research role, interns at Tower contribute directly to live trading systems.

Here, you’ll learn how ML powers decision-making in billion-dollar trading systems, blending math, programming, and high-stakes strategy. Apply for this internship here.

AI/ML Computer Vision Research Intern at Sony

Location: San Jose (USA), Domain: Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Image Processing.

Sony’s R&D labs are constantly pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with imaging and entertainment tech. As a Computer Vision Research Intern, you’ll work on 3D perception, multi-modal image understanding, and real-time object detection, directly contributing to future product innovations.

Here, you will get access to world-class labs and mentors working on deep vision models that will shape the next generation of smart devices, cameras, and XR. Apply for this internship here.

AI Engineer Intern at Boston Consulting Group (BCG)

Location: London (United Kingdom), Domain: Applied AI, Strategy, Responsible AI, Model Optimization.

BCG is where business meets machine learning. As an AI Engineering Intern, you’ll help build and optimize ML models for real business use cases, ranging from demand forecasting to sustainability optimization. You’ll also explore ethical AI principles and scalable deployment pipelines in a consulting environment.

Here, you’ll experience the intersection of AI and strategic impact, making this perfect for those who want to work on both the tech and business side of AI. Apply for this internship here.

AI Agent Developer Intern at Nokia

Location: Ottawa (Canada), Domain: AI Agents, Autonomous Systems, LLMs, Telecommunications.

Nokia is reinventing itself as a leader in network intelligence. As an AI Agent Developer Intern, you’ll help build autonomous agents that can optimize networks, detect faults, and perform tasks using LLM-based reasoning. The role offers exposure to cutting-edge agentic AI frameworks, ideal for students exploring next-gen applications of LLMs and reinforcement learning.

The fusion of LLMs and telecom infrastructure puts you at the forefront of building intelligent systems that think and act. Apply for this internship here.

GenAI Intern at Wedded Wonderland

Location: New South Wales (Australia), Domain: Generative AI, Creative Tech, AI Content Generation.

Wedded Wonderland is a top-tier lifestyle and wedding brand, and they’re leveraging Generative AI to revolutionize visual storytelling. As a GenAI Intern, you’ll build tools that generate personalized event content, style recommendations, and creative visuals using diffusion models and GPT-based systems.

This is where tech meets creativity. Perfect for anyone looking to apply AI in fashion, branding, or media with real-world creative impact. Apply for this internship here.

Summary

So, here are 5 of the best AI internship programs for Summer 2025:

  1. Machine Learning Intern at Tower Research Capital
  2. AI/ML Computer Vision Research Intern at Sony
  3. AI Engineer Intern at Boston Consulting Group
  4. AI Agent Developer Intern at Nokia
  5. GenAI Intern at Wedded Wonderland
