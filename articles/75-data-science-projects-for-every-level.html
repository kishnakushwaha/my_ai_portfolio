75 Data Science Projects for Every Level

Nov 08, 2025 • 5 min read
← Previous Article All Articles Next Article →

You’ve finished another online course and watched hours of tutorials, but you still don’t feel confident enough to apply for your first Data Science job. It’s a feeling that derails countless aspiring data scientists. This article serves as a roadmap to break through that barrier. In this article, I have curated a list of 75 hands-on Data Science projects designed to build a killer portfolio, sharpen your skills at every level, and turn your knowledge into a career.

Projects to Learn Data Collection, Pipelines, and Preparation

Every great data science project starts with great data. These projects will teach you the essential, non-negotiable skills for getting, cleaning, and organizing data at scale. Don’t skip these.

  1. Data Collection using APIs
  2. Web Data ETL Pipeline
  3. ETL Pipeline using PySpark
  4. Data Preprocessing Pipeline
  5. MLOps Pipeline using Apache Airflow
  6. B2B Courier Charges Accuracy Analysis

Once you have clean data, what stories can it tell? This category is all about asking the right questions, finding patterns, and communicating insights. It is the domain of the Data Analyst and the core of any strategic Data Science role.

  2. Netflix Content Strategy Analysis
  3. Instagram Content Strategy Optimization
  4. YouTube Data Collection and Analysis
  5. Financial Data Analysis
  6. Analyzing the Impact of Carbon Emissions
  7. Cohort Analysis
  8. RFM Analysis
  9. Analyzing Screen Time
  10. Supply Chain Analysis
  11. Build An Analytics Dashboard
  12. Rainfall Trends in India Analysis
  13. Election Ad Spending Analysis
  14. Metro Operations Optimization
  15. Electric Vehicles Market Size Analysis
  16. Impact of Inflation Analysis
  17. IPL 2024 RCB vs DC Analysis
  18. Batting Intent Analysis in IPL 
  19. Food Delivery Cost and Profitability Analysis
  20. Website Performance Analysis
  21. Audio Data Processing and Analysis

Projects to Learn Generative AI, LLMs, and Transformers

Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI have changed everything. Having projects in this space on your portfolio signals that you are current, ambitious, and ready for the future of AI.

Final Words

Seeing a list of 75 Data Science projects can be intimidating. Don’t let it be. You don’t need to do all of them. You need to do one. Pick a project weekly from this list that genuinely excites you. Each project you complete is a new story you can tell in an interview, a new skill on your resume.
