10 Generative AI Projects to Build for Your Portfolio

Building these projects isn’t just about filling up a GitHub repository. It is an exercise in empathy. When you make the Document Analysis tool, you are thinking about the paralegal drowning in paperwork.

Don’t just build the code; build the solution. That is what makes a portfolio stand out.
