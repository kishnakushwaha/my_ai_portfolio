AI Agents Projects for Resume

Oct 16, 2025 • 5 min read
← Previous Article All Articles Next Article →

Just like large language models, the use of AI Agents is increasing to automate tasks where decision-making is required. Hiring managers will love to see projects on your resume based on AI Agents that can learn, adapt, and improve over time. So, in this article, I’ll take you through some of the best projects based on AI Agents you should try to boost your resume.

AI Agents Projects for Resume

Below are some of the best projects based on AI Agents you should try to boost your resume. The projects mentioned below are solved and explained using Python as well.

AI Trading Agent using Reinforcement Learning

Build an AI trading agent that learns how to buy and sell stocks or cryptocurrencies by optimizing for maximum profit using Reinforcement Learning (RL). This agent should simulate a trading environment and use Deep Q-Networks (DQN), Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), or Advantage Actor-Critic (A2C) to improve its trading strategy over time.

It will help you demonstrate skills in AI decision-making in real-world finance and expertise in Reinforcement Learning & market prediction.

Find a solved & explained example of building an AI Trading Agent using Reinforcement Learning from here.

LLM-Based AI Agent to Generate Responses

Build an LLM-powered AI agent that generates human-like responses in a chatbot, customer support tool, or personal AI assistant. This project involves leveraging LLMs like GPT-4, LLaMA, or Mistral to create context-aware, intelligent conversations.

It will help you demonstrate expertise in LLMs & natural language understanding, prompt engineering & chatbot development skills, and AI Agent deployment for real-world applications.

Find a solved & explained example of building an LLM-Based AI Agent to Generate Responses by IBM from here.

Building an AI Research Agent for Image Analysis

Build an AI research agent that autonomously analyzes images, detects patterns, and generates insights. This agent can be applied in medical imaging, satellite imagery, or industrial defect detection.

It will demonstrate expertise in Computer Vision & AI automation, integrating AI agents into research workflows, and problem-solving in real-world image analysis tasks.

Find a solved & explained example of building an AI Research Agent for Image Analysis by IBM from here.

Summary

So, here are some of the best projects based on AI Agents you should try to boost your resume:

  1. AI Trading Agent using Reinforcement Learning
  2. LLM-Based AI Agent to Generate Responses
  3. Building an AI Research Agent for Image Analysis
